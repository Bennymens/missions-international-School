OUR MOTTO

"Arise and Shine"

OUR MISSION

To equip young Africans with Christ-Centered education that shapes strong character, ignites creative business leadership and empowers them to transform Africa's future.

MATTHEW 28:19-20 “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations baptizing them in the name of the Father of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit”, verse 20 says, and teaching them to obey everything (I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always to the very end of the age.

OUR VISION

To raise a new generation of African leaders who will rebuild Africa through unshakable faith, deep knowledge and divine wisdom.
