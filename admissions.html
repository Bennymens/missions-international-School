Scholarships Information

Mission International School is committed to making quality education accessible. We offer various scholarship opportunities for deserving students based on academic excellence, athletic achievement, financial need, and talent in the arts and leadership. Our scholarships can cover up to 50% of tuition, helping talented students from all backgrounds join our community. Click on any scholarship to learn about eligibility requirements.

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Minimum 3.8 GPA
  • Outstanding performance in standardized tests (SAT/ACT)
  • Strong letters of recommendation from teachers
  • Essay demonstrating academic passion and goals
  • Commitment to maintaining GPA throughout enrollment

Athletic Excellence Scholarship

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Demonstrated athletic excellence in recognized sports
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA
  • Coach recommendation and athletic assessment
  • Commitment to team participation and discipline
  • Video portfolio or competition records

Need-Based Aid

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Demonstrated financial need through FAFSA or equivalent
  • Minimum 2.5 GPA
  • Complete financial aid application and documentation
  • Family income assessment
  • Award amount customized based on individual circumstances

Arts & Leadership Scholarship

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Portfolio or audition demonstrating talent (music, drama, visual arts)
  • Leadership experience in school or community activities
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA
  • Teacher/mentor recommendation
  • Essay on artistic or leadership vision
