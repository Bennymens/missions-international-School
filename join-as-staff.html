Join MIS as a Staff Member

Thank you for your interest in joining the Mission International School team.

Mission International School is committed to attracting and retaining outstanding educators and support staff who share our passion for providing world-class education. We offer a collaborative, supportive work environment where professional growth and development are prioritised.

Our staff come from diverse backgrounds and nationalities, bringing a wealth of experience and perspectives to our school community. Whether you are an experienced educator or a support professional, MIS provides opportunities for meaningful impact in the lives of students.

MIS offers competitive compensation packages, professional development opportunities, and a vibrant community of dedicated professionals committed to excellence in education.

THE STAFF APPLICATION PROCESS

Stage 1: Application Submission

All prospective staff members are required to submit the following documents to the Human Resources Office:

  • A completed MIS Staff Application Form (available digitally through the Human Resources Office).
  • A detailed curriculum vitae (CV) highlighting relevant qualifications, experience, and professional achievements.
  • A cover letter expressing your interest in the position and how your skills align with the MIS mission and values.
  • Copies of academic certificates, professional qualifications, and teaching licenses (where applicable).
  • Contact details of at least three professional references, including your most recent employer.

Stage 2: Screening and Shortlisting

Applications are reviewed by the Human Resources Office in collaboration with relevant department heads. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for the next stage.

  • All applications undergo thorough background and reference checks.
  • Teaching candidates are required to hold recognised teaching qualifications and relevant subject expertise.
  • Support staff candidates should demonstrate relevant experience and skills for the position applied for.

Stage 3: Interview and Demonstration

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview process which may include:

  • A formal interview with the interview panel, which may include the Principal, department heads, and HR representatives.
  • Teaching candidates may be required to deliver a demonstration lesson observed by academic leadership.
  • International candidates may be interviewed via video conferencing at the discretion of the Principal.

Stage 4: Offer of Employment

The decision to make an offer of employment is made by the Principal in consultation with the relevant department.

  • Successful candidates will receive a formal offer letter outlining terms of employment, compensation, and start date.
  • Candidates must provide all mandatory documentation including police clearance, medical reports, and valid identification before commencement.

Stage 5: Onboarding and Orientation

New staff members participate in a comprehensive orientation programme designed to familiarise them with MIS policies, procedures, culture, and their specific role within the school community.

Interested? Send your application to missionsinternationalschool@gmail.com or visit our Careers page for current openings.

Teaching Staff: Classroom teachers, specialist teachers (Music, Art, P.E., ICT, Languages), department heads, and academic coordinators.

Support Staff: Administrative assistants, IT support, library staff, laboratory technicians, counsellors, nurses, and facilities management.

Leadership: Senior leadership positions are advertised as they become available and require significant experience in international education settings.

What qualifications are required for teaching positions?
Teaching staff must hold a recognised teaching qualification (e.g., B.Ed, PGCE, or equivalent) and relevant subject expertise. Experience with Cambridge International curricula is an advantage.

Does MIS offer professional development?
Yes. MIS is committed to continuous professional development and provides regular training opportunities, workshops, and support for further qualifications.

Can international applicants apply?
Yes. MIS welcomes applications from qualified professionals worldwide. The school provides assistance with work permits and relocation for international hires where applicable.
