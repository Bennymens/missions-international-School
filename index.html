Welcome to MIS

Mission International School is a not-for-profit, private, co-educational day school.
It was established in September 1955 to provide international education to students
of all races and creeds. Almost 70 years on, MIS continues to provide quality education
to both international and local students in a competitive setting.

MIS offers a curriculum based on the Cambridge International Early Years, Primary and
Secondary programs leading to the IGCSE and Advanced level examinations. We also have
a Pearson BTEC Pathway in Music and Creative Media.

Mission International School is accredited by both the Council of International Schools (CIS)
and the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC).
