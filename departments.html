Currently the library has over 18,000 resources. The collection is made up of fiction, non-fiction, reference books, magazines, scholarly journals, resourceful websites and videos which support the curriculum. Recently, the library completed subscription to the "Britannica On Line" Database and has also signed on to "JSTOR."

We continue to provide our students with resources to develop their knowledge and understanding of the world in which we live.

The Guidance and Counseling Department offers confidential advice to students and their families across the school from reception to the sixth form. The Department gives career guidance and assists with applications to universities and colleges both internationally and nationally.
