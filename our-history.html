Brief History

Mission International School (MIS) was officially birthed on 15th February 2015 with its grand opening ceremony. The school's journey began with a focus on early childhood development, launching its inaugural Preschool program to shape the foundation of young minds in a Christ-centered environment.

In September 2015, following the successful launch of the preschool, MIS expanded its educational reach by starting the Primary Program. This marked a significant step in the school's commitment to delivering a broad and rigorous curriculum across the formative years of childhood.

The school continued its steady growth over the years, reaching a major milestone on 7th September 2021 with the commencement of the Junior High School (JHS) Program. This expansion allowed MIS to continue its mission into secondary education, nurturing independent thinking and creative leadership.

Building on this momentum, the Senior High School (SHS) Program was launched on 23rd September 2024. As we look forward, the school community is set to celebrate its 10th Anniversary on 4th March 2025, marking a decade of "Arising and Shining" as a beacon of excellence.
